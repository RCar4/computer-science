My favourite holiday destination: Perth, Western Australia

Perth is my favourite holiday destinattion because there is alot of things to do there such as, off-roading in 4x4 trucks to camping and hiking, to walks through the valley of the giants in the walpole-nornalup national park.

What makes perth special

Perth has a large history shared with cork as the old prison there Fremantle prison was the prison that they used to send prisoners that were held in spike island prison to in order to make room in spike island, which gave perth alot of new labour to build the city. perth also has a rich mining background as kalgoorly the largest gold and iron mine in Australia is only a 3 hour flight east of Perth.
